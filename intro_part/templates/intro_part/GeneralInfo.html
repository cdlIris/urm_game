Welcome and thank you for your participation!

Please feel free to ask any questions during the experiment and an experimenter will help you privately. You may use the paper and pen on your desk, but not any other tools, such as calculator or smart phone. Turn off your phones or at least turn on the silent mode. Please remain quiet and refrain from talking to others or looking at others’ screens during the experiment. Thank you for your cooperation!

Today’s session contains 4 Blocks. Each Block contains 10 rounds. In each Round, you will observe some computerised ball drawings from some urns. You will then be asked to make some decisions based on the information we will provide to you. Finally, your payment will depend on your answers and luck. Understanding the following instructions will help you maximize your payoff. So please pay attention. The currency used in this experiment is called Experimental Cash Unit (ECU). Your ECUs will be converted to monetary rewards at the end at the rate of 30 ECUs=£1.

Please try your best in answering all questions, as any question may count to determine your final real cash payments. You will be paid at the end of the experiment in private. And you may ask any questions you might still have.

You can find an information sheet on your desk. It contains important information about the experiment and an example. Please refer to it whenever you need throughout the experiment.
