Before we begin, we will first go through a practice round which will not count towards your monetary payoff.

The top row shows the high Q urns were Q=8, and the bottom row shows the low Q urns were Q=5. The number of red urns in high-Q urns and the number of red urns in low-Q urns are always equal.
